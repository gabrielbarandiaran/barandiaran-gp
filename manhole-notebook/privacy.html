Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Manhole Notebook ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and verification)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Username

Collection Data: We store your manhole card collection, including which cards you've collected and when.

Location Data: With your permission, we access your location to show nearby cards. Location is processed locally and not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app experience
  • To sync your collection across devices
  • To send verification and account-related emails
  • To enable social features (if you choose to use them)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers in Japan. Card images and public data are served via Cloudflare's global CDN.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: For authentication (optional)
  • Resend: For sending emails
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and export your data
  • Delete your account at any time from Settings
  • Opt out of optional data collection
We do not:
  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing the service

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

Questions? Contact us at support@manholenotebook.com
