Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Manhole Notebook ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Username (required, public)
  • Display name (optional)

Collection Data: We store your manhole card collection, including which cards you've collected, favorited, wishlisted, and any notes you add.

Social Features: If you use social features, we store:

  • Posts you create (including captions and card references)
  • Location data you attach to posts (optional)
  • Friend connections and requests
  • Likes on posts

Device Information: We store push notification tokens to send you notifications (if enabled).

Location Data: With your permission, we access your location to show nearby cards. Location is processed on your device and not stored on our servers, unless you choose to attach it to a post.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app experience
  • To sync your collection across devices
  • To send verification and account-related emails
  • To enable social features (posts, friends, likes)
  • To send push notifications (new cards, friend requests, etc.)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers with encryption at rest and in transit. Card images and public data are served via Cloudflare's global CDN.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: For authentication (optional)
  • Apple (APNs) / Google (FCM): For push notifications
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and export your collection data
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings
  • Control who sees your posts (friends only or public)
  • Disable push notifications at any time
We do not:
  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Use analytics or tracking SDKs

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
